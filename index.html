MORE MOMENTS                                    

Dear Students, Parents, and Guardians,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our school community. As your principal, I am thrilled to be a part of a dedicated team of educators who are committed to providing our students with a quality education and a safe learning environment. Our school is a place where everyone is valued, respected, and supported in their academic, social, and emotional growth. We believe that each student has unique talents and strengths, and we are committed to helping them reach their full potential. As we embark on this new school year together, I encourage you to be involved in your child's education and to take advantage of the many opportunities that our school has to offer. Whether it's volunteering in the classroom, attending school events, or joining our parent-teacher association, your involvement is essential to the success of our students.

Thank you for entrusting us with the education of your child. I look forward to getting to know you and working with you to make this school year a great one.

Sincerely,



Mr. Bikram Subedi
Principal,DMS

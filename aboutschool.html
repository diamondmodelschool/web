Diamond Model School (DMS),is a non-profit institution founded in 2056 with the boarding school which offers its service to students ranging from Grades Nursery to 12.The school
is a multi-lingual, multi-cultural and multi-ethnic educational institution that incorporates all castes, cultures, religions, values, and languages.It features an inquiry based,concept
driven curriculum that provides a well-rounded,student-centered program. The language of instruction is English. Students are blessed with the opportunity to express their
talents in a variety of athletics, academic, artistic and creative work.The school sponsors a variety of clubs such as Health and Sanitation,
Handwriting, and English that can foster their potential. Seven academic departments and seven administrative sections
team up to work for a better output. A new Building and playground have recently been added to the School.





Mission & Objectives

WE BELIEVE THAT

  1. To provide an educational program that develops students curiosity and creativity.
  2. To develop responsible and engaged learners.
  3. To foster tolerance,respect and acceptance of others.
  4. To build each student’s resilience and self-esteem.
  5. To provide a curriculum that is rich and relevant and allows all students to succeed.
  6. To promote a strong, supportive partnership between home and the school and the wider community.
  7. To have high expectations of ourselves and others.
